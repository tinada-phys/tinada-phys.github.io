Research

Main interests

Quantum Field Theory in de Sitter

Correlators, wavefunction methods, vacuum structure, and consistency conditions in curved backgrounds.

Perturbative Unitarity Bounds

Theoretical constraints on effective theories, with applications to cosmology and inflationary settings.

Inflation and EFT

Early-universe model building, cosmological observables, and UV-sensitive aspects of inflationary dynamics.
